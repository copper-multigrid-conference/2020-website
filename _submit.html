Presentation Format

Presentations at the conference are featured in morning and afternoon session and are normally 25 minutes in length, including time for questions. Presentations are grouped generally by theme.

How To Participate

To submit an abstract for a presentation, please use EasyChair, the web link is given below. Please note that there is a limit of 1 talk per participant.

Submit an abstract

At the top of the submission page you can read the CM2020 submission instructions. At the bottom, you will be asked to submit to one of the two tracks:
  1. The CM2020 regular program that requires an abstract by Jan 15.
  2. The Student paper competition (see instructions and deadlines )
Choose the appropriate track and continue. Using the instructions provide the required information.
